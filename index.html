Why we made this blog?

Since 2021, I have been trying to learn web development. However, every time I tried my motivation ran out, and forgot about learning for a couple of weeks or even months. At that moment, it seemed that web development was not for me, so I let it go. At the end of 2023, I saw my library of courses and decided to give web development another try. Afraid of repeating the same mistakes as before and leaving things without completing them, I proposed to Aldo to start a blog together. I have known Aldo since High School, and he has always been outspoken, even more so after he committed to studying cinema. Now every time I open my social media, I see a post from Aldo with random thoughts, so I think he's the perfect partner to start this blog. After a long brainstorming session, we came up with the name, and now we are ready to start sharing our thoughts with you. This is only the beginning so let's see where this path takes us.
